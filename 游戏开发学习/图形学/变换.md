# 变换

## 二维变换

$$
\begin{aligned}
&x^{\prime}=s x \\
&y^{\prime}=s y
\end{aligned}
$$
$$
\left[\begin{array}{l}
x^{\prime} \\
y^{\prime}
\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{ll}
s & 0 \\
0 & s
\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{l}
x \\
y
\end{array}\right]
$$
不包括平移的变换
$$
\begin{aligned}
x^{\prime} &=a x+b y \\
y^{\prime} &=c x+d y \\
{\left[\begin{array}{l}
x^{\prime} \\
y^{\prime}
\end{array}\right] } &=\left[\begin{array}{ll}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{l}
x \\
y
\end{array}\right] \\
\mathbf{x}^{\prime} &=\mathbf{M} \mathbf{x}
\end{aligned}
$$
